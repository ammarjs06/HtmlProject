VISION

St. John’s College, situated in the heart of Tirunelveli, serves the encompassing community by its obligation to develop its stuff. We strive hard to lead the students of this institution from the darkness of ignorance into the light of knowledge and wisdom with a definite focus on steady academic achievements. We are much committed to transmit our own exclusive rich heritage down to generations, thereby exclusively devoted to transfigure lives. We, being unique, stand as creators of better civilization.

MISSION

  1. To facilitate the learning and acquisition of knowledge with the aid of modern technology and other available.
  2. To build the character and personality of the students with a strong moral and spiritual basis.
  3. To strengthen the teaching and learning skills and to maintain a good academic ambience.
  4. To create social responsibility and global consciousness through various external activities.
  5. To provide comprehensive adaptability to meet the diverse needs of the stakeholders.
  6. To encourage the faculty to identify new areas of knowledge and to update teaching, learning and motivating.
  7. To produce successful young minds for the betterment of the society.
